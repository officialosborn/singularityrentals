Terms & Conditions
1
Rent Payment: The Tenant agrees to pay the monthly rent of — on or before the 1st day of each month. Late payments attract a penalty of 5% per week overdue.
2
Advance Payment: The Tenant has paid an advance of — which shall be applied to the first months of the tenancy as agreed between both parties.
3
Property Use: The property shall be used solely for residential purposes. The Tenant shall not sublet, assign, or part with possession of the property without the written consent of the Landlord.
4
Maintenance: The Tenant shall keep the property clean and in good condition. Minor repairs under GHS 200 are the Tenant's responsibility. Major structural repairs are the Landlord's responsibility.
5
Utilities: Water, electricity, and other utility bills are the responsibility of the Tenant unless otherwise stated in writing by the Landlord.
6
Notice to Quit: Either party wishing to terminate this agreement must give one (1) calendar month's written notice to the other party. Failure to give adequate notice results in forfeiture of one month's rent.
7
Alterations: The Tenant shall not make any structural alterations to the property without prior written consent from the Landlord.
8
Access: The Landlord reserves the right to inspect the property with 48 hours written notice to the Tenant, except in cases of emergency.
9
Governing Law: This Agreement is governed by the Rent Act of Ghana (Act 220, 1963) and any disputes shall be resolved through the Rent Control Department of Ghana or a court of competent jurisdiction.

Digital Agreement: Both parties agree that this digitally signed agreement constitutes a legally binding contract equivalent to a physical signature under Ghana's Electronic Transactions Act, 2008 (Act 772).
